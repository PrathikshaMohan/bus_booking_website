About Us

"Dolphin Tours" is an innovative online bus ticket booking platform, envisioning a hassle-free and enhanced public transportation system in Sri Lanka. We offer Online bus ticket booking services 24 X 7, bus tracking facilities and fleet management services. Our payment gateways are secured. Once you reserve a seat with us you can change/modify subject to our seat cancellation policy. Furthermore, you will be promptly notified if a bus that you have booked has been changed or time schedules change. Everyone booking through us and passengers can reserve their seats one month ahead of the journey.
